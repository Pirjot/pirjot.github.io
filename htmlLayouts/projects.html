Projects

This app details some of the projects that I've completed over the years and a description for what each do! I also provide some ideas for projects that I'm looking forward to undertaking in the future (if I have the time).

AuxBattle

AuxBattle is my most recent project that I built with a couple of friends! Its an online replica of the popular game of the same name, which involves players going head to head in a bracket competition to find out who has the better music taste. It's right now in its beta version, but I'm looking to see if I can finalize it into a more official app that can possibly go on mobile devices.

MakeMyFuture

MakeMyFuture is a project that I completed while serving as the president of Dot-Slash Computer Science at Ohlone. When beginning the planning for the project, I had the idea to also include other STEM clubs on the development of the project so over the course of a few weeks, I presented the project to different clubs at Ohlone, including IEEE STEAM, Girls Who Code, Web Dev Club, and AI Club.

For two months, I led online classes to instruct my peers on the necessary background needed for web development, and over the course of a year I managed a team of builders to see the website finished. During Ohlone Hackathon 3.0, I showed off the website to Ohlone students to gain a starting audience for future years. Overall, I was very happy with the devleopment of the project.

MimirNet

MimirNet is my first official venture into the world of Artificial Intelligence. I've always been interested in new and upcoming AI and their employment in solving interesting problems. MimirNet is my first attempt at creating an Artificial Intelligence Engine. I didn't want to immediately jump into using AI libraries which are prebuilt (Tensorflow and the like) since I think starting from scratch can provide a better insight on using the abstracted version of the program. Thus, MimirNet is a simple dense layered forward propagating Neural Network using a sigmoid and mean squared error algorithm for optimization. Currently I'm in the process of building a project that uses the Tensorflow library, and hopefully you'll see that here!

GAPI Client-Side Script

My GAPI Client-Side Script is a clever trick I used to solve the issue of fetching Google Sheets data to someone who wasn't signed into an account that can access it. This was used to display the location data on a LeafletJS map for the various schools that NSTEM (the company I was interning at) was affiliated with.
